Use Skin and Custom Colors.

You can control the appearance of the buttons within the applet by specifying a new skin. A skin is a GIF or JPEG file containing images for the different button in various states. The skins shown in this example and others are vailable in your SDK in the "listenup/images" folder.

<param name="skin" value="../images/16buttonSkin.jpg">

You can also specify a new foreground and background color to match your web page. Use a 6 digit hex number with 2 digits each for Red/Green/Blue. Maximum for one color is FF.

<param name="background" value="E6E3D0">
<param name="foreground" value="807060">
<param name="waveBackground" value="D0D0FF">
<param name="waveForeground" value="804080">

Here is the Applet with a White background in a colored DIV. The bevel and logo do not work well with a white background so we turned them off.

<param name="skin" value="../images/DiddlySkin1.jpg">
