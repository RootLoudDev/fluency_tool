Recording Editor

You can enable basic waveform editing functions by setting the "editable" parameter. You also need to enable the waveform display. 

    <param name="showWaveform" value="yes">
    <param name="editable" value="yes">
Try out these basic editing functions:
  1. Hit the red Record button and record 5 to 10 seconds of audio.
  2. Hit the Pause button "||" then hit Record again. Notice that you can append to the end of a recording.
  3. Hit Pause then drag the mouse over a section of the waveform.
  4. Hit Play and notice that just the selection will be played.
  5. Hit Record and notice that the selection is replaced with newly recorded audio.
  6. Select a region and then right-click on the region. You will get a popup menu that lets you delete the selected region.
  7. Double click to select the entire message.
